Welcome to Computer Services Center

Computer Service Center(CSC) is one of the central facilities of the National Institute of Technology Srinagar.The work of CSC is based on the relevant laws and regulations issued by the National Institute of Technology Srinagar. You can contact the Computer Service Center by phone, email or by submitting a help request 24*7.



CSC Facility


Smart Data Center for NIT Srinagar

NIT Srinagar has setup a new Data Center in Computer Servies Center with smart racks. These racks house high end servers to host NIT's E-governance which includes Student Registrations, Fee payments, Payrolls, Guest House registrations, E-file tracking and the official website of NIT Srinagar. It also houses high end routers, Switches, firewall and RDU for monitoring smoke detection, rodents, fire, dust etc.

10G Internal Network Infrastructure

NIT Srinagar hign end switching is on its way to provide 10G Internal network infrastructure for students and staff.

Wifi|Campus Open Air Wifi

Campus Wifi facility is available for students aiming to provide seamless connectivity to all.

Smart Labs and Class rooms

NIT Srinagar houses high end workstations for research scholars and B.Tech/M.Tech students to carry out their research and project work.

e-Requisition system- A step towards e-governance

Computer Services Center has been providing help desk facility to address student, alumini and staff queries. These queries include LAN issues, user creation, domain registration, wireless network issues, hardware issues etc. CSC is also providing help to students and staff in other issues like operating system formatting, repairing of hardware, installation of software, UPS, LAN cables, switches stc.

Software Web Store-OCSM

Computer Services Center provides local software repository for students and staff.

VPN e-Resources|OFf Campus Access

CSC allows students and staff to use VPN services so that the resources are available to them 24*7.
