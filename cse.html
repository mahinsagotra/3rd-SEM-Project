Computer Science & Engineering

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was started in the year 2007 , offering four year undergraduate courses, wherein undergraduate students are imparted knowledge in multitude of specialitites,including computer Architecture Network , Operating System ,Data Structure and Algorithm.Theory of Computation ,Numerical analysis, Compiler design, Graphics , Artifical intelligence, Database Management System,Data mining ,Software Engineering ,Human computer interaction etc. The Department of Computer Science and Engineering embodes the Institutes tradition of Excellence as a word-class leader in computer Science and Engineering education and research.
