Civil Engineering

The department of Civil Engineering is the largest and one of the earliest Engineering departments of NIT srinagar established in the year 1960.It consists of a group of highly qualified and experienced faculty,supported by experienced technical staff and a good laboratory infrastructure.The department offers instructions at undergraduate level leading the 4 year B.TECH degree in Civil engineering and at the post graduate level leading to M.TECH degrees in water resources engineering,Structural engineering ,Geotechnical Engineering and Transportation Engineering and Planning and also PH.D degrees.
The instructions at undergraduate level aims at providing the students a broad based education with emphasis on theory and practice in Civil engizneering, keeping in view the current and future requirements of the industry.

Vision:


To create a unique identity of the Department by achieving excellent standards of quality technical education and keeping pace with the rapidly changing technologies and to produce Civil Engineers of global standards with the capability of accepting new challenges.



Mission:


M1.To promote academic growth in the field of Civil Engineering by offering state-of-the-art undergraduate and postgraduate programmes

M2.To provide knowledge base and consultancy services in all areas of Civil Engineering for industry and societal needs.

M3. To inculate higher moral and ethical values among the students to become competent Civil Engineers with overall leadership qualities.

M4. To establish a Centre of Excellence in the emerging areas of research related to Civil Engineering and its allied fields.
